Product overview

Make common directory lookups faster and easier to teach

AD SuperSearch is aimed at the everyday support and administration work that often starts with “can you look this user or computer up for me?” It turns those checks into a more approachable desktop workflow across both Windows and Mac environments.

Search across object types

Use one app to look up users, groups, and computers without juggling multiple tools.

Reduce heavy tooling

Give teams a simpler lookup experience when they do not want to live inside traditional admin consoles.

Support-friendly on both platforms

Built for repeated help desk and admin checks where speed and clarity matter in mixed-device environments.
