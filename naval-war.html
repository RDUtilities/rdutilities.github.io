Game overview

Fleet tactics, card-driven chaos, and luck at sea

Naval War combines strategy and luck through two decks: a Ship Deck filled with unique vessels and a Play Deck packed with attacks, defenses, and tactical surprises. Players use gun-size-matched salvo cards and special actions to damage or sink enemy fleets.

Command a fleet

Each player starts with ship cards and play cards, then works to protect their own fleet while targeting opponents with the right attacks.

Use tactical play cards

Salvos, minefields, submarines, torpedo boats, repairs, smoke, additional damage, destroyer squadrons, and carrier air strikes all create different paths to victory.

Win by survival or points

Skirmish mode rewards the last fleet afloat, while campaign play can score rounds by the hit-point value of ships sunk into each player’s victory pile.
